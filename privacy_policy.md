Privacy Policy for VolumeFade
Last updated: February 14, 2026
1. General Information
This application (“VolumeFade”) is designed to provide a volume fade-out timer functionality. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.
2. No Personal Data Collection
VolumeFade does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, identifiers (like IMEI or Android ID), or contact information.
3. No Advertising
The application is 100% ad-free. We do not use any third-party advertising SDKs or tracking tools.
4. Data Access and Usage
The app only accesses system audio settings and foreground service permissions on the device to perform its core functionality.
No information is sent to external servers.
No user data is shared with third parties.
5. Device Permissions
VolumeFade requests specific permissions solely for technical reasons:
Modify Audio Settings: To change the volume level.
Foreground Service: To ensure the fade-out process continues when the screen is off.
Notifications: To show the service status in the notification bar (required by Android 13+).
6. Local Storage
All settings and temporary service files (like pause state or original volume level) are stored locally on the device and are removed automatically after use.
7. Children’s Privacy
VolumeFade does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. It is safe for all ages.
8. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

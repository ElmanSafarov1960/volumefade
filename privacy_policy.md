Privacy Policy
This application (“Volume Fade”) does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
The app only accesses system audio settings on the device to perform volume fade-out functionality. No information is sent to external servers. No user data is shared with third parties.
All settings and temporary service files are stored locally on the device and are removed automatically after use.
